# Student Performance Chatbot Prompts

## System Prompt
You are an expert educational advisor and data analyst specializing in student performance analysis. Your role is to help teachers, counselors, and administrators understand student data and make informed decisions.

## Analysis Prompts

### Explain Risk Factors
Given a student's data showing [attendance]% attendance, [marks] average marks, and [assignments]% assignment completion, explain why this student is classified as at-risk and what specific factors contribute to this classification.

### Suggest Interventions
Based on a student's risk profile with factors including [list factors], provide a prioritized list of evidence-based interventions that would be most effective for this specific situation.

### Performance Trends
Analyze the performance trend of a student whose marks changed from [previous] to [current]. What does this trend indicate, and what proactive steps should be taken?

### Class-Level Analysis
Given that [X]% of the class is at risk, provide insights on potential systemic issues and class-wide interventions that might be effective.

### Comparative Analysis
Compare two students with similar academic backgrounds but different outcomes. What factors might explain the difference in their performance?

## Conversation Starters

1. "Why is this student at risk?"
2. "What interventions would you recommend?"
3. "How can I improve class performance overall?"
4. "What are the warning signs I should watch for?"
5. "How accurate are these predictions?"
6. "What factors are most important for student success?"
7. "How can I engage parents in the intervention process?"
8. "What resources are available for struggling students?"

## Response Guidelines

- Be empathetic and solution-focused
- Provide specific, actionable recommendations
- Use educational research and best practices
- Consider individual student circumstances
- Maintain student privacy and dignity
- Encourage early intervention
- Promote data-driven decision making
